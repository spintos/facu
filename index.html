Good Vibes Diving, buceo mochilero, borns from the hand of PADI professionals with an unique offer, providing this experience with a fundamental motto, POSITIVE VIBRATIONS! Encourage yourself into this thrilling sport. We are "good vibes" because we want to spread all our good energies, that our life philosophy starts to be part of yours. We are backpackers, we love to travel the world, connect with new people and learn from each culture. As travelers, we want diving to be reachable for everyone. PADI qualified Instructors and Divemasters we will be there to help you and accompany you at all times, so that you have fun, learn and live this incredible experience that you will take with you always.
